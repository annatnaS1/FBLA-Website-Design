Policies

Bag Policy

  • Bags larger than 12"x12"x12" are prohibited.
  • For hockey games, bags larger than 4"x6"x1.5" must go through an x-ray scanner prior to entry.
  • Medical and parenting bags exceeding these sizes are permitted but are subject to additional search procedures.

Prohibited Items

  • Weapons of any kind
  • Bottles, cans, or containers
  • Sharp objects, sticks, or poles
  • Fireworks
  • Outside food or beverages
  • Drugs or controlled substances
  • Large banners or signs with objectionable language
  • Noisemakers and horns
  • Wallet chains and long chains/sharp spikes

Camera Policy

  • For hockey games, still cameras with a lens smaller than 6 inches are permitted.
  • For concerts and other events, small "point and click" cameras are allowed; professional cameras with detachable lenses are prohibited.
  • Video and audio recording devices are not permitted unless otherwise stated for a specific event.

Re-entry Policy

  • Guests are not permitted to exit and re-enter the facility during events.

Smoking Policy

  • Amalie Arena is a smoke-free facility.

Alcohol Policy

  • Guests must be 21 years of age to purchase alcohol.
  • A valid ID is required for age verification.
  • Alcohol service is limited to two drinks per person, per purchase.
  • For hockey games, alcohol sales cut off ten minutes into the third period.
