Amenities

Dining Options

  • Cur(ate) TPA: A culinary experience featuring fresh, local, and sustainable food and beverage options available at multiple concession locations around the arena.
  • Market On Four: Includes five local Tampa eateries—Bake'n Babes, Bavaro's, King of the Coop, Malio's, and Zukku Sushi—offering a diverse culinary journey.
  • Consessions: Serves items such as hot dogs, pizza, and fries.

Premium Seating and Hospitality

  • Club Seats: Offer all-inclusive food, mixed drinks, beer, wine, and soda, plus VIP parking. Amenities include upscale dining, private club access, and concierge services.
  • Hospitality Suites: Provide a private setting with upscale food and beverage service, ideal for entertaining clients or enjoying events with a group.
  • ConnectWise Loge: A membership-only, all-inclusive concept featuring private seating, an exclusive lounge, and upscale food and beverage service.

Family and Accessibility Services

  • Family Restrooms: Two family restrooms are available, located behind sections 208 and 326, equipped with standard toiletries and baby-changing tables.
  • First Aid: Two first aid rooms are located across from sections 112 and 312, staffed by medical professionals to assist with any health concerns during events.
  • Guest Services: Located on the main concourse across from section 101, offering assistance with various needs, including wheelchair escorts, lost and found, and sensory bags.
